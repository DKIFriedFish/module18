My Work



Project Beauty Share

Non-governmental organization website redesign. This is a group project by Matthew Pardede (me), Nomita Nanduri, Sevilya Ilyasova, Tahsina Zaman, Alena Pavlova for our UX Design Bootcamp at Columbia University.

The brief was to redesign a non-governmental website. Project Beauty Share® provides personal hygiene, cosmetics and beauty products to non-profit organizations who serve women and families overcoming abuse, addiction, homelessness and poverty to help restore hope and dignity in their lives.

MyCloset App

An app for users to plan outfits digitally using clothes in their actual wardrobe. This is a group project by Matthew Pardede (me), Jade Wang, Tahsina Zaman, and Kylie Smith for our UX Design Bootcamp at Columbia University.

People frequently cite that the biggest difficulties when getting dressed is considering all the factors that go into their outfit. My Closet enables busy users to plan ahead to reduce decision fatigue and increase willpower throughout the day.
